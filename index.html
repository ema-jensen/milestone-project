ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Ema Jensen. My little family and I currently reside in Eugene, Oregon. I spend most of my days creating and designing websites intertwined with chasing my babies, human and canine. In the minutes that I have to myself, I love journaling, breathing through a yoga class or finding a house project to start working on.

I am very persistent and pay close attention to detail. Problem solving can be very frustrating but I enjoy the challenge and finding the solution is always worth the struggle. Managing time and different projects is a talent of mine. Learning the way that people interact with digital mediums is something that intrigues me. I love creating simple and user-friendly platforms for all types of creators, businesses and artists.
